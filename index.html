The 1-4-7 Spaced Repetition Rule — Applied Every Single Day
Learn a topic on Day X. Revise it on Day X+3 (the "4th day" touch) for 1–2 hrs with MCQs. Revise again on Day X+6 (the "7th day" touch) for 1–1.5 hrs with problem solving. Every morning session starts with the mandatory 1-4-7 revision block before any new learning begins.
